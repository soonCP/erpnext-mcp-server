Integrative Medicine Psychiatry PC — 2025 Payroll Documents
===========================================================

IMPC uses two payroll providers:

1. Justworks (PEO — Professional Employer Organization)
   Justworks is the employer of record for payroll tax purposes.
   Payroll taxes (941s, W-2s) are filed under Justworks' EIN, not IMPC's EIN.

2. Rippling (payroll processor, not a PEO)
   Rippling processes payroll but IMPC is the employer of record.
   Payroll taxes are filed under IMPC's EIN.
   Rippling payroll auto-syncs to QuickBooks Online via integration.

Justworks Files:
- IMPC_Justworks_Invoice_Details_2025.csv — Full year invoice detail from Justworks showing gross wages, employer taxes, benefits, and fees billed to IMPC.
- Form_941_IMPC_2025_Q1-Q4.pdf — Quarterly Form 941 reports allocated to IMPC by Justworks.
- W2_2025_[Employee].pdf — Individual W-2 forms for each employee, issued by Justworks.
